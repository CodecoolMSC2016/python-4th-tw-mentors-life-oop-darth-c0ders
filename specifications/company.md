# Company

## Description
This class represents companies who hire students to work after their programming course.
You can find more details at our Trello site. (https://trello.com/b/82EL1p6I/mentors-life).

## Parent class
None

### ```apply_for_company```

The class should have a method, which check student knowledge and energy level (arguments) at random values: 70 <= energy, knowledge < 100,
and print students name who are engaged by companies.
